{{ en ? 'Personal Introduction' : '个人简介' }}

我叫杜蓓越，可以叫我Britt。


在我10岁生日时，我收到了人生中第一台数码相机，从那以后，我通过镜头看到了世界的其它模样， 也看到人生更多可能性，也因此我选择了CUC的Digital Media这个专业进行更进一步的学习。


在校期间，我的课外经历进一步使我接触到数字媒体的影响力。作为CUC电视台技术部的团队负责人和BILIBILI上大学官方帐户的共同创始人。经过多次智能手机和数码相机的试验，我和团队成员们共同解决了从数码单反相机到网站的连接问题，并获得了更好的实时流媒体效果，目前获得了超过16w的粉丝。同时，我参与了2016-2018年三年的学生记者节晚会直播工作，在此期间应用了多种数字媒体动画，成功吸引了50,000多名观众。


除了校内的实践活动，我凭借出色的摄影作品，2016年被一家旅行平台陀螺旅行选拔为摄影师。我用相机记录所见的风景和人，并通过互联网和更多人分享。听到来自世界各地肯定的声音，我第一次感受到传播的魅力，于是热爱旅行的我创建了个人旅行账号，在帐号上分享照片和旅行攻略，慢慢的受到了更多人的认可（详情可见社交媒体页面）。


在数字媒体时代如何更好地表达自己？如何被更多人看见？成为我不断思考的问题，我尝试通过不同渠道寻找答案。


通过在中国传媒大学的学习，我受到了非常好的多媒体技术的教育，我也有幸去很多地方，感受不同的文化，用旅行VLOG记录自己对世界的探索。在尊重不同文化差异的同时，我也发现在媒体科技和网络媒体发展越来越快、全球化的今天，没有国家能真正阻止媒介的流通。


作为一个数字媒体的制作、传播者，我希望自己能通过影像探索世界，更进一步的了解传播领域的奥秘。

My name is Du Beiyue, you can call me Britt.


I received my first digital camera on my 10th birthday. Since then, I have seen world appearances through the lens, and I have seen more possibilities in life. Therefore, I chose to major in Digital Media, communication university of China for further study.


During my college life, my extracurricular experience further exposed me to the influence of digital media. As the team leader of the technical department of CUCTV and the co-founder of the official university account of BILIBILI. After many experiments on smart phones and digital cameras, The team members and I worked together to solve the problem of connecting from digital SLR cameras to the website, and got better real-time streaming effects. Currently, we have won more than 160,00 fans in CUC account. At the same time, I participated in the live broadcast of the Student Reporter's Party from 2016 to 2018. During this period, I applied a variety of digital media animations and successfully attracted more than 50,000 viewers.


In addition to the practical activities on campus, In 2016 I was selected as a photographer by a travel platform called Torro Travel with outstanding photography. I use the camera to record the scenery and people I see and share it with more people through the Internet. I heard the affirmation from all over the world.For the first time, I felt the charm of communication. So created a personal travel account because I loved travel. I shared photos and travel tips on my account, and I was slowly recognized by more people. (See the social media page for details)


How to better express yourself in the digital media era? How to be seen by more people? Becoming a problem that I keep thinking about, I try to find answers through different channels.


Majoring in Digital Media Arts in Communication University of China, I have been educated in very good multimedia technology. I have travelled to many places, embracing different cultures and using vlogs to record my exploration of the world. I found that media technology and online media are developing faster and more globally nowadays. None of country can really stop the circulation of media.


As a producer and disseminator of digital media, I hope that I can explore the world through images and further understand the mysteries of communication.。
